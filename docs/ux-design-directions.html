Which Design Direction Resonates Most?

Quick Comparison:

Direction 1: Chart-First

Visual empowerment. Charts above the fold. Balanced density. Best for "magic moment" emphasis.

Direction 2: Summary-First

Data-dense. Numbers first. Compact for power users who want more info visible.

Direction 3: List-Focused

Input-focused. Transaction list dominant. Charts secondary. For logging-first users.

Direction 4: Card-Based

Friendly & spacious. Cards everywhere. Breathing room. Best for beginners.

Direction 5: Split-View

Professional & dense. Side-by-side layout. For power users on tablets/desktop.

Direction 6: FAB-Centered

Minimalist. Giant FAB. Action-first. Ultra input-focused. Simple & clean.
